Dutch Cheat Sheet

Goal: Have the lowest card score at the end of the round. Call "Dutch!" when you think you have achieved this.

Card Scoring Values:

  • Ace: 1 point
  • 2 - 10: Face value
  • Jack / Queen: 10 points
  • Black King: 10 points
  • Red King: -2 points (Jackpot!)
  • Joker: 0 points

Special Card Abilities:

  • 7 or 8: Look at 1 of your own cards.
  • 9 or 10: Look at 1 opponent's card.
  • Black J or Q: Blindly swap any 1 of your cards with an opponent.
  • Black King: Look at 1 of your cards AND 1 opponent's card, then swap if you want.

Snapping:

If the top discarded card matches any card on the board, you can click it instantly to discard your match out-of-turn!
