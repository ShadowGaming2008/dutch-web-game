📖 How to Play

Goal: Bankrupt every other player. Last tycoon standing wins.

Your Turn

Roll the dice to move around the board. Land on an unowned property, railroad, or utility and you can buy it or send it to auction. Land on someone else's and you'll owe rent automatically. Rolling doubles gives you another turn — but three doubles in a row sends you straight to Jail.

Rent

Owning every property in a color group doubles the base rent, even with no houses built. Railroad rent scales with how many railroads you own; utility rent is a multiple of the dice roll (4× with one utility, 10× with both).

Building

Own every property in a color group and you can build houses (then a hotel at 5) to raise the rent on that space. Houses must be built evenly across the group — you can't stack them on one property while others in the set lag behind.

Auctions

Decline to buy a property you land on and it goes to auction — every other player gets a turn to bid or pass, and the highest bidder wins it from the bank.

Mortgaging

Need cash? Mortgage a property (with no houses on it) for half its price. Mortgaged properties collect no rent until you pay back the mortgage value plus 10% interest to unmortgage.

Jail

Landing on "Go To Jail" sends you straight there. Escape by paying a $50 fine, rolling doubles (up to 3 attempts before the fine is charged automatically), or using a Get Out of Jail Free card if you've drawn one.

Bankruptcy

If rent or tax drops your balance below $0, you're out — your properties return to the bank and you're skipped for the rest of the game. Once only one player is left, they win.
